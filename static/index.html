Welcome to the Thesis Demo

This is the start page for Group Signatures and Anonymous Credentials demo server.

Available Endpoints

  • /group/ – Group signature schema
  • /cred/ – Anonymous credentials schema
  • /group/keygen – Group key generation
  • /group/sign – Group sign
  • /group/verify – Group verify
  • /group/open – Group open
  • /cred/setup – Credential setup
  • /cred/issue – Credential issue
  • /cred/present – Credential present
  • /cred/verify – Credential verify
